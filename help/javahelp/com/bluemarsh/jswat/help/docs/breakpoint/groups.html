Editing Breakpoint Groups

See Also

Changes to existing breakpoints groups can be made by selecting the group in the Breakpoints window and right-clicking on it and selecting Customize from the context menu. A dialog featuring the breakpoint group customizer will be displayed. When you are done, click Ok to save your changes.

Groups and Conditions

As with breakpoints, it is possible to set a boolean expression to act as a condition for a breakpoint group. This condition applies to all of the breakpoints contained in that group. So in order for a breakpoint to suspend the debuggee, not only must the breakpoint's conditions be satisfied, but so must the conditions of the breakpoint's group. Likewise, the conditions of the parent of the breakpoint group must also be satisfied, and so on, up to the top-most breakpoint group. For this reason, it is a good idea to avoid setting conditions on the Default group. Otherwise you may wonder why none of your breakpoints are stopping when you expect them to.
